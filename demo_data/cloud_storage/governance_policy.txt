GCP DATA GOVERNANCE & PRIVACY COMPLIANCE POLICY (2026)
------------------------------------------------------
1. All datasets containing PII (Direct Identifiers: Name, Email, Card Numbers) must be tagged with template 'Privacy Compliance'.
2. Production financial transaction tables must maintain classification='Confidential' and retention_days=2555 (7 years).
3. Vertex AI Model Feature Stores and Dataplex Data Streams must be tagged with template 'AI Governance' prior to model deployment.
